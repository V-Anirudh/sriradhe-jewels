Shipping Policy

Last updated: {{ lastUpdated }}

1. Shipping Coverage

Sree Radheya Jewellers currently ships across India. All shipments are fully insured for the total value of the jewellery being shipped. We ensure secure and tamper-proof packaging for every order.

2. Shipping Charges

Orders above ₹25,000 Free Insured Shipping
Orders below ₹25,000 ₹500 flat rate (insured)
In-store Pickup (Hyderabad) Free

3. Processing Time

  • Ready-to-ship items: Dispatched within 2–3 business days after order confirmation
  • Made-to-order items: 7–15 business days depending on design complexity
  • Custom jewellery: 15–30 business days (timeline communicated during design consultation)

You will receive an email/SMS notification once your order is dispatched with tracking details.

4. Delivery Timelines

Hyderabad (Local) 1–2 business days
Metro Cities 3–5 business days
Other Locations 5–8 business days
Remote/Rural Areas 8–12 business days

* Delivery timelines are estimates and may vary due to unforeseen circumstances.

5. Packaging & Security

Every order from Sree Radheya Jewellers is:

  • Packaged in a premium branded jewellery box
  • Sealed with tamper-evident security packaging
  • Shipped via trusted and insured logistics partners (BlueDart / DTDC / FedEx)
  • Requires signature upon delivery for security
  • Accompanied by a BIS hallmark certificate and detailed invoice

6. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your order directly on the courier partner's website or by contacting our customer support team.

7. Delivery Issues

In case of any delivery issues, please note:

  • Damaged Package: Do not accept the delivery. Refuse the package and contact us immediately.
  • Missing Items: Contact us within 24 hours of delivery with your order details.
  • Incorrect Address: Please ensure your shipping address is accurate. Re-shipping charges may apply for returned packages due to incorrect addresses.
  • Non-delivery: If your order has not arrived within the estimated timeframe, contact us and we will initiate a trace with our courier partner.

8. In-Store Pickup

For customers in Hyderabad, you may choose to pick up your order at our store. You will be notified when your order is ready. Please bring a valid government-issued photo ID for verification at the time of pickup.

Store Address: 1-18-4/2 Plot No 6, Dr. AS Rao Nagar, Hyderabad
Hours: Mon – Sat: 10 AM – 8 PM | Sunday: 11 AM – 6 PM
