✦ Our Story

A Fresh Chapter in Indian Jewellery

Sree Radheya Jewellers was born from a simple belief — that fine Indian jewellery should be accessible, transparent, and crafted with uncompromising purity. Conceived in 2025 and launched in 2026 in Hyderabad, we set out to build a jewellery brand that the modern customer can trust, one that combines time-honoured Indian craftsmanship with a contemporary design sensibility.

As a young brand, we bring fresh energy and new ideas to the art of jewellery making. Every piece in our collection is BIS hallmarked, ethically sourced, and handcrafted by skilled artisans — because we believe that even as a new name, our commitment to quality speaks louder than years.
