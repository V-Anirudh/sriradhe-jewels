✦ Our Heritage

A Legacy Woven in Gold

Born in the vibrant city of Hyderabad, Shree Radheya Jewellers began as a humble workshop where Master Goldsmith Shri Radheshyam ji transformed precious metals into wearable art. His vision was simple yet profound — to create jewellery that honours India's rich artistic heritage while meeting the highest standards of purity and craftsmanship.

Today, three generations later, we continue that legacy with the same devotion. Every piece that leaves our atelier carries the soul of centuries-old techniques — from the delicate art of Kundan setting to the vibrant beauty of Meenakari enamel — reimagined for the modern connoisseur.
