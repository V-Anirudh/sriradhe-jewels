Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: {{ lastUpdated }}

1. Our Commitment

At Sree Radheya Jewellers, we want you to be completely delighted with every purchase. If for any reason you're not satisfied, our return and refund policy is designed to make the process as smooth as possible.

2. Return Eligibility

Return Window 15 days from delivery
Condition Unworn, original packaging, tags intact
Certificate Required BIS hallmark certificate & invoice

3. Items Eligible for Return

  • Gold jewellery (necklaces, earrings, bangles, rings, mangalsutra)
  • Diamond jewellery (subject to inspection)
  • Jewellery sets and bridal collections

4. Items NOT Eligible for Return

  • Custom-made jewellery: Personalised or made-to-order pieces
  • Altered items: Resized, engraved, or modified jewellery
  • Damaged items: Items damaged due to misuse, wear, or improper storage
  • Loose gemstones & coins: Sold separately from jewellery
  • Sale/discounted items: Items purchased during promotional offers (unless defective)

5. Return Process

  1. Initiate Return: Contact us via phone (+91 9010800400) or email (Connect@sreeradheyajewellers.com) within 15 days of delivery
  2. Verification: Our team will verify your order details and issue a return authorisation
  3. Ship or Visit: You may ship the item back (insured) or visit our store in Hyderabad for an in-person return
  4. Quality Inspection: Our experts will inspect the returned item within 2–3 business days
  5. Refund Processed: Upon successful inspection, your refund will be initiated

6. Refund Options

Store Credit 100% of item value — Processed within 2 business days
Gold Exchange Full gold value at prevailing rate
Bank Transfer / Original Payment Processed within 7–10 business days (making charges may be deducted)

* For bank transfers, making charges and stone-setting charges (if applicable) may be non-refundable.

7. Exchange Policy

We offer hassle-free exchanges! You may exchange your purchase for another item of equal or greater value. If the new item costs more, you only pay the difference. Exchanges can be done at our Hyderabad store or through our customer support team.

8. Defective or Damaged Products

If you receive a defective or damaged product, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the item and packaging. We will arrange a free pickup and provide a full replacement or refund at no extra cost.

9. Cancellation Policy

  • Before Dispatch: Orders can be cancelled free of charge before they are shipped
  • After Dispatch: Cancellations after dispatch will be treated as returns once delivered
  • Custom Orders: Custom orders cannot be cancelled once production has begun
